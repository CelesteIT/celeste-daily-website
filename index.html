About Celeste

Built for convenience, trusted for quality.

Celeste, Sri Lanka’s largest and fastest growing on-demand online supermarket, was first founded in 2020 during the Covid-19 pandemic to fulfil the basic requirements of the community by delivering fresh produce and essentials.

Celeste was founded by the visionary leader Janik Jayasuriya, introducing the on-demand grocery shopping experience to Sri Lanka and revolutionizing the quick commerce market through Uber Eats and PickMe Market. As a proud local brand, Celeste has now become the lifestyle partner of Colombo, delivering anything you need to your doorstep in under 30 minutes, any time of the day, as we are open 24/7, 365 days a year.
