Celeste Internship Programme

The Celeste Internship Programme is designed for students, school leavers, undergraduates, and freshers who want practical exposure to how a modern retail and quick-commerce business operates.

You will get the chance to support real teams, learn live workflows, and understand how outlet operations, fulfilment, customer service, digital platforms, and business support connect together.

Good fit for

  • Students and undergraduates
  • Freshers starting their career
  • People looking for practical work exposure

Exposure areas

  • Retail and outlet operations
  • Fulfilment and platform support
  • Marketing, admin, and digital support

Long Term Team Player

This path is for people who want to build a long-term career with Celeste, take responsibility, support daily execution, and become part of the company’s next stage of growth.

We value people who are dependable, clear in communication, willing to learn, and ready to take ownership. The work is practical and fast-moving, so attitude matters as much as skill.

Good fit for

  • People looking for stable long-term growth
  • Reliable team players
  • People who can handle responsibility

Possible work areas

  • Retail operations and outlet support
  • Fulfilment and platform coordination
  • Customer experience and business support
