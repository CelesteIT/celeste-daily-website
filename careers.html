Why Careers at Celeste

A workplace where execution matters more than theory.

Celeste operates in a space where speed, accuracy, availability, customer experience, and team coordination all matter every day. This gives our people exposure to how modern retail and quick-commerce actually work behind the scenes.

We are building a team for the long run. That means we value reliability, communication, willingness to learn, and the ability to take ownership without waiting for everything to be pushed.

Career Areas

Where you can build experience with Celeste.

Our teams support both the visible customer experience and the operational backbone that keeps Celeste moving.

Retail Operations

Outlet execution, stock coordination, product availability, order readiness, and location-level operational support.

Fulfilment Operations

Brand store support, fulfilment flows, picking readiness, partner location coordination, and platform-driven execution.

Quick-Commerce Platforms

Supporting platform visibility, order flow, product listings, marketplace coordination, and daily platform performance.

Customer Experience

Customer communication, issue handling, service follow-up, and improving the quality of the Celeste experience.

Digital & IT Support

Website updates, digital workflows, internal tools, reporting support, system coordination, and tech-enabled operations.

Brand & Business Support

Campaign coordination, product content, admin work, supplier communication, documentation, and business support tasks.
