Our Story

From a moment of need to a growing retail movement.

Celeste Daily is a Colombo-based on-demand online supermarket founded in 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic, with the goal of making grocery shopping faster, safer, and more convenient. Operating 24/7, the company delivers a wide range of products from fresh produce to everyday essentials directly to customers’ doorsteps, often within 30 minutes through platforms like Uber Eats and PickMe Market.

As one of Sri Lanka’s fastest-growing quick-commerce brands, Celeste Daily has served over 100,000 customers and completed millions of orders, positioning itself as a leading digital retail solution that combines speed, reliability, and a modern shopping experience.

Founder

Janik Jayasuriya

Janik Jayasuriya is a Sri Lankan entrepreneur and the Founder and Managing Director of Celeste Daily, launched in 2020 during the COVID-19 pandemic. With a background in hospitality management, he identified the growing need for reliable, on-demand grocery delivery and transitioned into the retail and quick-commerce space.

What began as a community-driven effort to connect farms with customers in Colombo has since grown into a leading online supermarket platform. Through innovation and a strong focus on convenience, Janik continues to drive Celeste Daily’s mission of delivering a faster, smarter, and more dependable retail experience.
